Font Stack

Sora

Sora is a sans serif typeface commissioned as a custom solution for branding and interface design. Sora works with blockchain technologies in diverse financial contexts and the typeface had to manifest, without any doubt, ideals of technology and engineering which are at the core of their business.

Inter

Inter is a versatile typeface designed especially for screens. It's an excellent choice for general user interface needs, but can also work well for body copy and headlines.
